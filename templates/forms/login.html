NIT Warangal introduced the Distinguished Alumnus Award program to honour outstanding former students and to celebrate their achievements. The purpose of the Distinguished Alumnus Award is to recognize the instituteâ€™s alumni, who have made significant contributions to the society. The Distinguished Alumnus Award will be an exceptional honour to its recipients. These awards will be given for outstanding achievement and there will be no compromise in diminishing the significance of the awards. Each award includes a citation and a certificate.

Award Categories:
 Recipients will be identified in the following three distinct spheres among two age groups, i.e.
   1. For Senior Alumnus (Age: above 40 years i.e. born before 01-01-1981)
     A. Distinguished Alumnus Award for Professional Excellence (DAAPE)
     B. Distinguished Alumnus Award for Public Service (DAAPS)
     C. Distinguished Alumnus Award for Service to the NIT Warangal (DAASN)

   2. For Young Alumnus (Age: Less than / equal to 40 years i.e. born on / after 01-01-1981)
     A. Distinguished Alumnus Award for Professional Excellence (DAAPE)
     B. Distinguished Alumnus Award for Public Service (DAAPS)
     C. Distinguished Alumnus Award for Service to the NIT Warangal (DAASN)

  3. Alumnus Lifetime Achievement Award (Age: above 60 years i.e. born before 01-01-1961)
